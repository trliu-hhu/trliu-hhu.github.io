Dr. Liu received the B.Eng. degree in automation and the D.Eng. degree in electronic information via a continuous direct doctoral program under the supervision of Professor Chen Yangyang from Southeast University, Nanjing, China, in 2020 and 2025, respectively. He joined Hohai University in 2026.



Dr. Liu is currently serving as an active reviewer for top-tier journals including IEEE/CAA Journal of Automatica Sinica, IEEE Transactions on Systems, Man, and Cybernetics: Systems, IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics, and IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems. He serves as an active reviewer for leading conferences such as the IEEE Conference on Decision and Control (CDC), American Control Conference (ACC), and European Control Conference (ECC). He served as Special Session Co-Chair of several flagship international conferences including ISCSIC 2026.



Dr. Liu's research interests include cooperative and intelligent control theory and its applications in multi-agent systems and automated vehicles. He has published 15+ fully-refereed papers in prestigious journals and leading conferences. Among his fully-refereed publications, 9 are high-quality journal articles, including 2 articles in Automatica and IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control (the two best journals in the area of automatic control) and 4 articles in the other IEEE Transactions and Magazine (the most prestigious journals in the fields of engineering and computer science).



Dr. Liu's research work has been cited 60+ times with h-index of 5 and i10-index of 2 according to Google Scholar, 40+ times with h-index of 5 according to SCOPUS and 30+ times with h-index of 4 according to Clarivate Analytics Web of Science Core Collection.
